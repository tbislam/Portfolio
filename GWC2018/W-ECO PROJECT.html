An Orangutan's View

120,000! That’s a rough estimate of how many orangutans are left in the world.

As more and more palm oil plantations are destroying rainforests, the more orangutans are suffering. Since palm oil is in so many products such as: shampoo, soap, baked goods, cookies, etc, the demand for it is really high. When the orangutan’s homes are destroyed they look for food on the plantations, but then they are tortured and killed because the workers see them as agricultural pests. But why is it a crime to look for food in the area that was forcefully taken away from you?

What's the New Fashion Trend for Sea Animals?

Is wearing a tight necklace fun? Not really, so then do you think this seal is having fun?

Over 100,000 sea animals die from plastic entanglement or plastic consumption. Even eating a piece of plastic that is as small as your pinky nail is deadly for sea animals. But it’s hard for them to stop themselves when there is about 100 million tons of plastic being thrown into the ocean.

Everyone Comes Together

Floods, wildfires, heat waves and ice caps melting are just the start of global warming.

We have the solutions to combat global warming; however, not everyone is trying. But now is the time for everyone to come and work together and try to save our Earth. Everyone has the ability, tools and resources to have a smaller carbon footprint, we just have to use them. The Earth helps to provide us with food, shelter, and many other resources, so shouldn’t we do everything we can to help preserve it.

Brands

Buy a reusable water bottle! 20,000 plastic water bottles per second are sold totalling 500 billion per year... Most end up in the ocean as 91 percent doesn't get recylced.

Use energy efficient light bulbs instead of regular bulbs. It will save you electricity, last longer, and save you money!

Get involved with organizations like 350.org and conservation.org where you can join rallies and volunteer in different ways

While having your skincare routine, go for the organic face wash which is paraben-free, sulfate-free, and cruelty-free.

Create a homemade compost bin to prevent waste going in landfills and you can provide the soil with nutrients. It will make your kitchen smell better and you will have less garbage

Wash your Laundry in cold water instead of hot. It saves energy and prevents carbon pollution. Hot water uses 90% energy while cold water uses 10%

Use public transportation a few times a week to avoid driving and regularly clean out your car to avoid making it heavier with junk. A heavier car will use more gas andmake sure your tires have enough air in them.

Save water by fxing leaks, taking short showers, turn offf the tap when brushing your teeth, and boil only the water you require for cooking

Ditch plastic bags. 100 bllion are used daily and only 1-3% are recycled.

About the Creators

Alexandra Kusio

My name is Alexandra Kusio and I am going into my Senior year.The inspiration for the project stemmed from my love for the environment. Growing up in Poland, I was constantly surrounded by nature and sustainable development. The goal with this project is to really raise awareness about how a single individual can have such a huge impact on nature in both good and bad ways.

I think that coding has allowed me to explore multiple mediums of how to get this message across. I have always loved the environment and with this modern touch of coding I was able to combine these two fields to make it more accessible to people from all over. In addition to coding, I also make documentaries.

Fun Fact: I am currently working on a documentary about my school and the problems we have with recycling.
